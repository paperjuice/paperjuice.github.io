Vault

In "Vault" I store some of my most important projects which helped me immensely to improve as a game developer but are not as polished as the games featured on the "Project" page.
Most of the games in "Vault" are developed with a focus on achieving something specific such as sprite sheet animations or 3D asset optimisation for mobile devices. Because my targets for these projects were for me to discover, learn and improve certain skills, some games are not available to play mainly because they don't offer any kind of game-play.

Idle grim

Period of creation: October 2014 - December 2014
Platform: PC
Engine: Unity Engine (ActionScript)
Source: Coming soon
Summary: "Idle grim" is an idle-game in which the player has to slain enemies while keeping their health up.
Investing in both damage and armour (the two categories in which currency can be invested) will allow the game to idle for a longer period of time.
When health is depleted, the damage drops significantly.
My main focus on the project was programming and game-play designing as part of a two man team.
While the project is very minimalistic, it did help me get started with games programming.
Also, "Idle Grim" is the first potent game that I worked on.

Mad Road

Period of creation: January 2016
Platform: Android Phone
Engine: Unity Engine (C#)
Source: Coming soon
Summary: "Mad Road" is a endless-runner game in which you have to dodge environment and from time to time battle foes in order to stay on track and increase your score.
My main focus with this project was optimisation. It was my first 3D game aimed for smart-phone and getting the right balance between graphics quality and performance was a valuable exercise.
The game managed to run smoothly even on older Androids (e.g. LG L7), The reason why is not on the "Projects" page is because I didn't carried on with the game development in order to offer a complete experience and have it available for the public.

Maria Pale

Period of creation: June 2015
Platform: PC
Engine: Unity Engine (C#)
Source: Coming soon
Summary: "Maria Pale" is a project that was heavily relying on art.
I am enthusiastic about every aspect of game development and so I was very curious how sprite-sheet animation works, what are the tools and some of the techniques used. While this is not by far what an knowledgeable person would create (pixel-art wise), I had the opportunity to peek an eye on the concept and work-flow.
Also, 2D character programming was something new for me at that time.

Soul Lust

Period of creation: October 2015 - January 2016
Platform: PC
Engine: Unity Engine (C#)
Source: YouTube gameplay
Summary: The mechanic in "Soul Lust" is something I've came up with and I really enjoy: the main character's health goes down every moment and the player is caught in this frenzy, non-stop action. The game-play sessions are short in order to keep the player focused and active.
On this project I worked only on programming, camera and particle effects as part of a two men team.
